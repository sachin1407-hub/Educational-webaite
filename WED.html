KIET GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS

Krishna Institute of Engineering and Technology, Ghaziabad, popularly known as KIET Ghaziabad, is a private institution approved by the AICTE and affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. Established in 1998, it is recognised by the NBA and SIRO, and is accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC. In 2025, Collegedunia ranked KIET Ghaziabad 260 in the engineering category

Krishna Institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs that lead to two primary degrees: B.Tech and M.Tech. The institute offers a B.Tech program in eleven specialisations. Admission to the B.Tech program is through the JEE Main score, followed by UPTAC counselling. For 2024, the JEE Mains overall cutoff rank for the general category ranged from 423501 to 1262568. The B.Tech program at KIET Ghaziabad has a total fee of around INR 4.48 Lakhs.

KIET Ghaziabad placement report 2025 for KIET Ghaziabad has not been released yet. As per the placement reports 2024, the median package was 4.5 Lakh per annum. Out of 1261 registered students, 1019 were placed, securing an 80 % placement record. More than 100 companies took part in the placement drive. Some of the top companies were Accenture, Meta, Amazon, Google, Apple, Microsoft, Netflix, Accenture, HCL, Wipro and many more
